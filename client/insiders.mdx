---
title: Insiders
description: Retrieve insider holders, institutional and fund ownership, major holders breakdowns, and insider transaction history.
---

The `client.insiders` namespace provides detailed ownership data for any publicly listed ticker, covering individual insiders, institutions, funds, and aggregate ownership summaries.

## Methods

### `individuals`

Returns a list of individual insider holders (officers and directors) and their share counts.

<ParamField path="ticker" type="string" required>
  The stock ticker symbol (e.g. `"AAPL"`).
</ParamField>

```python
individuals = client.insiders.individuals("AAPL")
for holder in individuals:
    print(holder["name"], holder["position"], holder["shares"])
```

---

### `institutions`

Returns institutional ownership data including holder names, share counts, and percentage held.

<ParamField path="ticker" type="string" required>
  The stock ticker symbol.
</ParamField>

```python
institutions = client.insiders.institutions("MSFT")
for inst in institutions[:10]:
    print(inst["organization"], inst["pctHeld"])
```

---

### `funds`

Returns mutual fund and ETF ownership data.

<ParamField path="ticker" type="string" required>
  The stock ticker symbol.
</ParamField>

```python
funds = client.insiders.funds("NVDA")
for fund in funds[:10]:
    print(fund["organization"], fund["shares"])
```

---

### `ownership`

Returns a high-level major holders breakdown, including percentages held by insiders, institutions, and the public float.

<ParamField path="ticker" type="string" required>
  The stock ticker symbol.
</ParamField>

```python
ownership = client.insiders.ownership("TSLA")
print(ownership["insidersPercentHeld"])
print(ownership["institutionsPercentHeld"])
print(ownership["institutionsFloatPercentHeld"])
```

---

### `transactions`

Returns a history of insider buy/sell transactions filed with the SEC.

<ParamField path="ticker" type="string" required>
  The stock ticker symbol.
</ParamField>

```python
transactions = client.insiders.transactions("AAPL")
for tx in transactions:
    print(tx["filerName"], tx["transactionText"], tx["shares"], tx["value"])
```

---

### `activity`

Returns net share purchase activity, summarizing whether insiders are net buyers or sellers over recent periods.

<ParamField path="ticker" type="string" required>
  The stock ticker symbol.
</ParamField>

```python
activity = client.insiders.activity("AMZN")
print(activity["netSharesPurchasedOverPeriod"])
print(activity["netPercentInsiderShares"])
```

<Note>
  Insider transaction data is sourced from SEC Form 4 filings. There may be a short delay between a filing event and its availability through the API.
</Note>

## Example: Full workflow

```python
from axion import Axion

client = Axion(api_key="your_api_key_here")

ticker = "GOOGL"

# High-level ownership split
ownership = client.insiders.ownership(ticker)
print(f"Insider %: {ownership['insidersPercentHeld']}")
print(f"Institution %: {ownership['institutionsPercentHeld']}")

# Top 5 institutional holders
institutions = client.insiders.institutions(ticker)
for inst in institutions[:5]:
    print(inst["organization"], inst["pctHeld"])

# Recent insider trades
transactions = client.insiders.transactions(ticker)
for tx in transactions[:5]:
    print(tx["filerName"], tx["transactionText"], tx["value"])

# Net buy/sell summary
activity = client.insiders.activity(ticker)
print(activity)
```

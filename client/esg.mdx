title: ESG
description: Access Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) scores for publicly listed companies.
---

The `client.esg` namespace provides ESG ratings and component scores for companies. ESG scores help evaluate a company’s exposure to long-term sustainability risks and ethical practices.

## Methods

### `data`

Returns ESG scores for the given ticker, including total ESG score and individual environmental, social, and governance component scores.

<ParamField path="ticker" type="string" required>
  The stock ticker symbol (e.g. `"AAPL"`, `"MSFT"`).
</ParamField>

```python
esg = client.esg.data("AAPL")
print(esg["totalEsg"])
print(esg["environmentScore"])
print(esg["socialScore"])
print(esg["governanceScore"])
print(esg["esgPerformance"])  # e.g. UNDER_PERF, AVG_PERF, OUT_PERF
```

<Info>
  ESG scores are typically updated on a periodic basis (quarterly or annually) and may reflect data from the most recent reporting period.
</Info>

## Return fields

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| `totalEsg` | Composite ESG risk score |
| `environmentScore` | Environmental risk component score |
| `socialScore` | Social risk component score |
| `governanceScore` | Governance risk component score |
| `esgPerformance` | Performance label relative to sector peers |
| `peerCount` | Number of peers used in comparison |
| `percentile` | Company’s percentile rank within its peer group |

## Example: Full workflow

```python
from axion import Axion

client = Axion(api_key="your_api_key_here")

tickers = ["AAPL", "MSFT", "GOOGL", "AMZN"]

print(f"{'Ticker':<8} {'Total':>6} {'Env':>6} {'Social':>6} {'Gov':>6} {'Performance'}")
print("-" * 55)

for t in tickers:
    esg = client.esg.data(t)
    print(
        f"{t:<8}"
        f"{esg.get('totalEsg', 'N/A'):>6}"
        f"{esg.get('environmentScore', 'N/A'):>6}"
        f"{esg.get('socialScore', 'N/A'):>6}"
        f"{esg.get('governanceScore', 'N/A'):>6}"
        f"  {esg.get('esgPerformance', '')}"
    )
```

<Note>
  Lower ESG risk scores generally indicate less exposure to ESG-related risks. Score interpretation may vary by data provider methodology.
</Note>

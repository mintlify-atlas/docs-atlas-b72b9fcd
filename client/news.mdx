---
title: News
description: Fetch general, company-specific, country-specific, and category-specific news articles.
---

The `client.news` namespace provides access to financial news articles across multiple dimensions — global headlines, company mentions, geographic focus, and topic categories.

## Methods

### `general`

Returns a stream of general financial news articles. No parameters required.

```python
articles = client.news.general()
for article in articles[:5]:
    print(article["title"])
    print(article["publisher"])
    print(article["link"])
    print()
```

---

### `company`

Returns news articles that mention a specific company.

<ParamField path="ticker" type="string" required>
  The stock ticker symbol (e.g. `"AAPL"`, `"TSLA"`).
</ParamField>

```python
aapl_news = client.news.company("AAPL")
for article in aapl_news[:5]:
    print(article["title"], article["providerPublishTime"])
```

---

### `country`

Returns news articles focused on a specific country.

<ParamField path="country" type="string" required>
  ISO country code (e.g. `"US"`, `"GB"`, `"JP"`, `"DE"`).
</ParamField>

```python
us_news = client.news.country("US")
jp_news = client.news.country("JP")

for article in us_news[:5]:
    print(article["title"])
```

---

### `category`

Returns news articles for a specific topic category.

<ParamField path="category" type="string" required>
  Category name (e.g. `"technology"`, `"energy"`, `"markets"`, `"crypto"`).
</ParamField>

```python
tech_news = client.news.category("technology")
energy_news = client.news.category("energy")

for article in tech_news[:5]:
    print(article["title"], article["publisher"])
```

<Note>
  Use `company()` for ticker-specific news monitoring and `general()` for broad market headline feeds.
</Note>

## Example: Full workflow

```python
from axion import Axion

client = Axion(api_key="your_api_key_here")

# Global headlines
headlines = client.news.general()
print(f"{len(headlines)} general articles")

# Watch a specific company
nvda_news = client.news.company("NVDA")
for article in nvda_news[:3]:
    print(f"[{article['publisher']}] {article['title']}")

# Regional focus
eu_news = client.news.country("DE")
print(f"{len(eu_news)} Germany-focused articles")

# Topic feed
crypto_news = client.news.category("crypto")
for a in crypto_news[:3]:
    print(a["title"])
```
